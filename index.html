FsLemming

An F# message-passing tutorial. Every lemming and the world is its own message-passing agent (an F# MailboxProcessor). Pick a skill, then click a lemming to assign it. Get enough to the green exit before time runs out. Wide levels scroll — right-drag the board, drag the minimap, use ← →, or hover the canvas edges.
